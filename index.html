Hello, I’m George Washington

My home is Mt. Vernon, VA.

Background

I was an apprentice surveyor in my youth and spent that time learning and developing a passion about the natural environment of my native Virginia.

I’ve been a professional solider in the British Army, raising to the rank of Colonel by the age of twenty-two. I served as Commander-in-Chief of the Continental Army and was one of the Founding Fathers.

Goals

If elected President, I plan to:

  • Reduce the Debt
  • Peace treaties
  • Establish a National Capital
  • Better relations with Great Britain
  • Trade

I’d like to work for the people of The United States to get this country off to a great start.
